What to Know about Blood Donation

Donating blood is a safe process and people should not hesitate to give or receive blood. Right now, eligible and healthy donors are strongly urged to make an appointment to give soon. 1 donation can potentially save up to 3 lives.Blood is the most precious gift that anyone can give to another person — the gift of life. A decision to donate your blood can save a life, or even several if your blood is separated into its components — red cells, platelets and plasma — which can be used individually for patients with specific conditions.

About Us

Our aim is to enable the individuals who want to donate blood and help needy.The person who need blood can communicate with the donor through the data which is availabe in the website.The main objective for developing the website is to educate the community on the benifits of Blood Donation, develope a Web-Based Blood Bank System to manage records of donors and recipients, and encourage voluntary blood donation, easily accessing any information about donors.
